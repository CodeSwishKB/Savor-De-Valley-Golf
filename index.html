Welcome to Savor De Valley Golf

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Valley Golf course, Savor de Valley Golf Restaurant is a haven for foodies seeking the perfect blend of exceptional cuisine and breathtaking views. With a menu featuring a range of sumptuous dishes, including authentic Filipino cuisine, Savor de Valley Golf is the perfect destination for those looking to indulge in a truly unforgettable dining experience. From its fresh, locally sourced ingredients to its impeccable service, Savor de Valley Golf is the perfect place to unwind, relax and savor every bite.
