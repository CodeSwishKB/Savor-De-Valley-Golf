About Us

Our History

April 27, 2023, our restaurant began its journey as a mere school project in our ICT program. Our instructor tasked us to create a website that would showcase the restaurant that we envisioned to build. With a simple combination of HTML elements, CSS rules, and a little bit of JavaScript, six students from Grade 12 ICT - Kyle, Ruffa, Althea, JC, Lloyd, and Justine - worked together to create a website that would eventually become the foundation of our restaurant's online presence. These students put together a menu list that featured a variety of delectable food items, refreshing drinks, and mouth-watering desserts that would be available at our restaurant. What started as a school project has now become a reality, and we can't wait to see what the future holds for our restaurant.

Mission

"At Savor de Valley Golf, our mission is to offer a unique dining experience that combines the pleasures of excellent cuisine with the breathtaking beauty of the golf course. We are dedicated to providing our guests with the highest quality of service, using only the freshest ingredients to create delicious dishes that appeal to a variety of tastes. We strive to foster an inviting atmosphere where guests can relax and enjoy our signature dishes, refreshing beverages, and stunning views of the golf course. Our goal is to provide an unforgettable experience for all of our guests and to create a welcoming community for golfers and food enthusiasts alike."

Vision

"At Savor de Valley Golf, we envision a restaurant that becomes the premier destination for golfers and food lovers. We strive to create an atmosphere that is warm and inviting, where guests can enjoy a unique dining experience amidst the natural beauty of the golf course. Our vision is to be known for our exceptional quality of food, our outstanding service, and our dedication to creating an unforgettable d ining experience for every guest. We aim to establish ourselves as a cornerstone of the community, providing a place where golfers and food enthusiasts can come together and enjoy the best of both worlds. We believe in building lasting relationships with our guests, and we are committed to exceeding their expectations every time they visit."
